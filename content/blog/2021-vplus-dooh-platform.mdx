<Section title="The Company" badge="Overview">

**Founded V+ Publicidad** (Santiago, Chile, 2019-2021) as Founder & Principal Engineer.

**Concept:** Uber-like experience for digital advertising. Drivers earn revenue by displaying ads on rear-window IoT devices.

**Business Model:**
- Drivers install IoT display devices on rear windows
- Platform serves real-time targeted ads based on location/time
- Drivers earn passive income
- Advertisers get mobile, measurable campaigns

</Section>

<Section title="Technical Architecture" badge="Full-Stack">

Built **complete end-to-end platform** as sole technical lead:

**IoT Devices:**
- Android-based rear-window displays
- Custom device programming (C + Python + Java)
- Real-time ad updates under high load
- Power management for all-day operation

**Web Platform (Vue.js / Nuxt.js):**
- Ad agency management panel
- Campaign creation and targeting
- Real-time analytics dashboard
- Advertiser billing system

**Mobile App (React Native):**
- Driver onboarding and management
- Earnings tracking
- Device pairing and configuration

**AWS Infrastructure:**
- Elastic Beanstalk for scalable web apps
- Lambda for serverless functions
- Route 53, CloudFront, S3
- AuroraDB (PostgreSQL) and DynamoDB
- Real-time ad serving at scale
